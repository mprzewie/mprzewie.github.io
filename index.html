About me

I am a PhD Student at the Jagiellonian University. I work with GMUM research group, under the supervision of prof. Jacek Tabor and prof. Marek Śmieja. I am also a member of the IDEAS NCBR Computer Vision group, led by prof. Tomasz Trzciński.
Research-wise, I am interested in efficient deep learning, particularly in low-data regimes. I work on topics such as:
  • Meta-Learning - how to train models to learn novel tasks.
  • Few-Shot Learning - how to train models which can learn from very small amounts of data.
  • Self-supervised Learning - how to learn good model initializations from unlabeled data?
